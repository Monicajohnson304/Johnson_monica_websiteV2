OUR STORY

Kasama, a bakery and modern Filipino restaurant, founded by chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores, graces Chicago's East Ukrainian Village. Their culinary journey melds tradition with innovation, offering a vibrant menu that celebrates Filipino flavors. From comforting Adobo to crispy Lechon Kawali, every dish tells a story of heritage and passion. Join us at Kasama for an unforgettable dining experience that captures the essence of Filipino cuisine. Welcome to our table!

OUR FAVORITES

Delicious Pho Noodle Soup

Our Pho Noodle Soup is a culinary masterpiece, featuring rich broth infused with aromatic spices, tender slices of chicken, delicate rice noodles, and a selection of fresh toppings like bean sprouts, Thai basil, and lime. Dive into a bowl of comfort and flavor, crafted with authentic ingredients and served with warmth and care.

Creme Fraiche Cheese Cake

Creamy and indulgent, our Crème Fraîche Cheesecake is a delightful blend of tangy cream cheese and velvety crème fraîche atop a buttery graham cracker crust. Finished with a luscious topping, it's a heavenly treat for your taste buds.

Pancit Perfection

Savor our authentic Pancit, a tantalizing Filipino noodle dish featuring thin rice noodles, fresh vegetables, tender meat or shrimp, and savory soy sauce, delivering a flavorful taste of Filipino tradition.
